Personal Data

Personal Data means any information that can identify you as an individual or indirectly identify you via Personally Identifying Information. Information that can directly identify an individual without further processing is defined as personal data. Examples are the name, a number or an IP address. For example, while setting up an account, an individual’s email and country data is collected, and the account is given an ID number so when sharing information with third parties it does not expose the customer.

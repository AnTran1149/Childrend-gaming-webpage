Reason for collecting data
Transaction Data

When you want to make a purchase on a website you need to give your credit card info as the following: name, address. credit card number, expiration date and security code. Later with this data the website will start processing the payment through the service provider that you choose so the transaction can be completed. During this process your service provider also receives data if there is any risks and to complete the payment.

Reason for collecting data
Using the Website

When you are on a website your device and browser information is collected and you might be tracked (depending on the website) to ensure that you are not a bot.

Reason for collecting data
Tracking Data and Cookies

Cookies are used to analyse how you use a website to improve the ads/ show ads that may interest you and to enhance the website usage. Some websites automatically accept the cookies, however there are also other websites which asks you whether you want to accept it or not. If you refuse the cookies, you may be denied access to the page. Cookies are also used to recognize you (on your device or on another device), or your device. Some websites collect data in other ways as well. For example, Google Analytics. The information which is collected from Google Analytics are stored on servers by Google in the United States. The data that is collected by Google is for evaluating the website, activity and internet usage for website operators.

Reason for collecting data
Violations

Certain data is also collected to detect if there is any violation and to prevent fraud. If any violation is detected the data that is collected will be stored for longer possible legal actions.

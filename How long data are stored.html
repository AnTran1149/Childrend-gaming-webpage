How long data are stored?

Your information will be stored for as long as it needed in serving the purpose of collecting and processing or for the storage and retention period required by law where the applicable law provides for longer storage and retention period. After that your Personal Data will be deleted, blocked or anonymized, as provided by applicable law. In particular:

  1. Account Termination: If you decided to terminate your account, all of your Personal Data will be deleted immediately.
  2. In certain cases Personal Data cannot be completely deleted in order to ensure the consistency of our company’s operation such as bug-report will not be deleted; rather, your contribution will be permanently anonymized.
  3. Under statutory commercial and tax law, our company is required to retain certain transactional data, this act can last up to 10 years.
  4. Family Data: if you withdraw your consent which related to your family information such as parents or child, we will delete every data respectively without delay.

Who has access to data?

  1. Our company and our subsidiaries will have fully right to access to your Personal data to serve the purpose which listed above. In the event of a reorganization, sale or merger, your Personal Data may be transferred to the third party who responsible for it under applicable laws.
  2. We may share your Personal Data to relevant third party who co-oporate in customer service support. Your data will be used under this Privacy Policy and as long as it needed for customer services.
  3. Because of several internet standard, we may share relevant information such as your IP address to the network provider.
  4. Your account includes message boards, forums and/or chat areas data. If there is any posted that against your will, please contact our customer service to remove it.
  5. If you link your account to a new third party, we may share your Personal Data according to your agreement between you and that party. In this case, we are not responsible for any violation related to which we have shared.
  6. We may release Personal Data to comply with court orders or laws and regulations that require us to disclose such information.
